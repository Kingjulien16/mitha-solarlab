About My Work

From physics simulations to instant AI predictions.

Running one TCAD device simulation takes minutes to hours. A trained model answers in milliseconds. That is the gap Mitha SolarLab closes.

🔬

Simulate

Thousands of perovskite solar cell designs were simulated in TCAD, sweeping geometry, doping, defects and resistances to map how each choice shapes efficiency.

🧠

Learn

Five machine learning models — and one weighted ensemble — were trained and tuned with 5-fold cross-validation on the simulation data. The best reaches R² = 0.9998.

⚡

Predict

Now any new device design gets an instant efficiency estimate. SHAP analysis shows the interface defect density (DitMid) is the strongest single lever on performance.

The Solar Cell

A perovskite device, layer by layer.

  • ITO front contact — transparent electrode; its doping and electron affinity are model inputs.
  • ZnO electron transport layers (ETL1 / ETL2) — thickness and surface recombination velocities matter.
  • Perovskite absorber — where light becomes charge; bandgap (Eg), thickness (tAbs) and defects (DitMid) dominate.
  • Hole transport layer (HTL) — carries holes to the back; LUMO alignment is tracked.
  • Silver back contact — with shunt & series resistance closing the circuit.
